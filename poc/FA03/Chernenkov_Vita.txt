  * Vita and Bibliography
Born in 1956, in Moscow Reg. Graduated from the Department of Automatic,
Tele-Mechanic and Electronic of the Moscow Technical University
of Communications and Informatics as radio-engineer.
1980-1984 - study on a post graduate course in Images Processing,
Scientific supervisor  - Prof. V.N.Ulianov.
Then I carried out  University scientific investigations for the Special Astrophysical Observatory
of Russian Academy of Sciences, where I work now as a senior researcher of
Informatica Department. 1993-1996 - study on a course in Radio Astronomy.
Scientific supervisor  - Prof. Y.N.Parijskij.
1996 - defended PhD thesis on Facilities for the development of a multilevel
territorially distributed data acquisition and processing system of the radio
telescope RATAN-600.
Participation in the project "Russian Virtual Observatory".
My work experience: investigation of radio sources
(http://cats.sao.ru/);
System programming with C, Perl, bash, SQL, Java, Python,
HTML; FITS format implementation for the SAO RAS observation data;
information system and network design for the SAO RAS: CATS, SED;
support of Internet Cisco Systems Co. equipments.

My home page: http://www.sao.ru/hq/vch/
Publications: about 100 papers, theses, reports.

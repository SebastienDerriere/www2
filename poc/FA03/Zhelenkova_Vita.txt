  * Vita and Bibliography
Born in 1955, in Grozny. Graduated from the Mathematical and Mechanical Department
of the Leningrad State University in 1978, as astronomer. Then I carried out
scientific investigations for the Special Astrophysical Observatory
of Russian Academy of Sciences, where I work now as a researcher of
Informatica Department. I am is a member of the European FITS Committee since
2000. Participation in the project "Russian Virtual Observatory".
My work experience: investigation of radio sources
(http://sed.sao.ru/rc_archive.html); data processing with MIDAS, Starlink;
programming with Fortran, C, Perl, bash, SQL, JavaScript,
HTML; FITS format implementation for the SAO RAS observation data; seach
information system design for the SAO RAS observation archive.
My home page: http://www.sao.ru/hq/zhe/.
Publications: about 120 papers, theses, reports.

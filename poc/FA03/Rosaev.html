DELPHI PROGRAM  FOR THE DETECTION OF PHOTOMETRIC INHOMOGENEOUS DETAILS

AT THE CELESTIAL BODY’S IMAGES

 

Rosaev A.E., Esipko O.A., Kolbasova A.V.

 

(1) FGUP NPC "NEDRA" Svobody 8/38, Yaroslavl 150000, Russia

 

The software for image analyses, based on Delphi 6, developed at FGUP NPC "NEDRA"  Yaroslav, Russia, is presented.

By using this program, analysis of some celestial bodies images, with the target to detect photometric contrast areas on their surfaces, is done. All considered images can be classify by degree of contrast. An example of low contrast is the image of Annefrank asteroid by Deep Space mission. In this case the contour map of isophotos, which reflect main details of Annefrank shape, is constructed. In case high contrast image, the increase of small details contrast is possible. So, at the developed image of Phobos, the inhomogeneous structure of the inner wall of the largest satellite’s crater is clear, and famous furrows are continued to satellite’s limb.

The program can be used for the test of images of the terrestrial surface for the purpose to study varied ecological and cartographic problems. In some, the most simple cases, aside from writing output information in bmp-file, the conservation of the result in the vector format is possible.
